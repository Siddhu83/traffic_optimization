🎯 Problem Statement: “AI Based Junction Optimization System”
Traffic congestion at road junctions is a common issue in many cities. Most traffic signals work on fixed timing systems, even when traffic levels are different on each side of the junction. Because of this, vehicles spend unnecessary time waiting, roads get crowded, and emergency vehicles also face delays.
Your task is to build an AI-based system that can improve traffic management at junctions in a smarter way.
Your solution can include features like:
• Detecting vehicle traffic in real time
• Adjusting signal timings automatically based on traffic density
• Giving priority to ambulances or emergency vehicles
• Predicting traffic congestion before it becomes serious
• Monitoring junction data through a dashboard
• Suggesting better traffic flow or lane management
You are free to add your own ideas and features as well.
The main focus is to create a smart and practical solution using AI that can help reduce traffic problems and improve junction efficiency.
